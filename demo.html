Cooper Hewitt Interpol

About

The Cooper Hewitt Interpol typeface is a variant of the Cooper Hewitt Typeface, drawn by Chester Jenkins. All the curves has been programmatically replaced by a fixed number of straight line.


You can download the font on framagit, gitlab or github, or have a glance at police-interpol, the collection of programs which have generated the font (also on framagit, gitlab or github).


Feel free to send me an email if you use it !
